Fantasy Metals!
Metal Description
Adamantium: A metal created by the Marvel Cinimatic universe! It is virtually indestructible, not naturally occuring, and is what Wolverine's bones are made out of!
Celestial Bronze: A metal in the Percy Jackson universe! It is one of the few metals that can harm monsters, and is what Percy's sword is made out of.
Mithril: Created by J.R.R. Tolkein, first appearing in The Lord of the Rings and is now widely found in various magical settings, from books to games to movies!
Brightsteel: Brightsteel is what the dragonrider's swords are made of in the The Inheritance Cycle.One such sword is made for Eragon in the later books.
Beskar: Known in Star Wars as one of the few materials that can resist lightsaber blades, Beskar is used for Mandalorian armor.
Please leave any suggestions for more metals in the survey form! Thank you!
