Orichalcum is mentioned in ancient writings, mainly those of ancient greece and rome. It was probably either a precious metal that was later renamed, or a type of brass or bronze.

Corinthian Bronze was one of the most valuable alloys in ancient greek times. It is thought to be alloyed with precious metals like silver and gold, or just a very high quality type of bronze.

Damascus Steel was used mainly for sword blades, as it was very tough and resistant to shattering. It looks very distinctive with a wavy patterns of lines on it.
