Welcome to the Mythical Metals Website!

The purpose of this website is to have a fun look at various metals, both from actual myths, and from fiction and fantasy.
Examples of mythical metals are Mythril and Orichalcum, and examples of fictional metals are Adamantium from the marvel universe, and Dilithium from Star Trek.
I would also appreciate it if you took the time to fill out the survey form at the end.
